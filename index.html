Morning Star High School (Barangabari)




About the school


Morning Star High School,Barangabari was launched in 1994 at Barangabari. It extends its Senior Secondary Section for the students of class XI, in the year 2020. Barangabari has the Heritage of Schooling since 1890. The first School was the Senior Basic School latterly known as Barangabari MV School crossed 120 years of glorified past.
